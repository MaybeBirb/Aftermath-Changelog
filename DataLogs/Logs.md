## Unsteady Alliance
```
----------------
Digital log entered by a PCA officer piloting an AR99 ARCHAENGEL.
The pilot has been identified as Capitol Warrant Officer Elias Thorne, the head of security at the HC manufacturing plant.
It seems to have been entered one day before the destruction of the machine.
----------------
I should jot this all down in case we need to call it in to the System, which... f#cking hell, I hope we never have to.

Incident report 19... or, a baseline, anyway. I'll get to the details later.
Three days ago we were attacked by MDF forces. Fought them for about 5 minutes before it showed up.
It destroyed the ENFORCER instantly- we had to call a ceasefire just to repel it.
It's still on the radar, so we and the MDF have formed an temporary alliance at this base until it’s killed.
Until then, we and the MDF forces on the scene are allies... for now.

So... we're just living with the Magmosians now, waiting for it to strike again.
And no, we don't like each other... at all.
Soren already tried flirting with one of them as a joke. Paid with his life. We're at wit's end.

I and the Magmosian lieutenant sat down and had a peaceful conversation... found ourselves agreeing completely and utterly on one thing.
Magmosians and Humans cannot possibly coexist.
There is no peaceful, cooperative relationship.
There never will be.
----------------
```

## State Of The War
```
----------------
Digital log entered by an MDF soldier.
Translated from the casual, Japanese-like sub-language of Magmosians.
----------------
Say what you will about Severon's timidness; he wouldn't have started this war the way Vaux did.

Vaux has his merits. He's a stronger and more effective leader than Severon could ever be.
But Vaux isn't as old or as wise. As content as Severon is to lead the Venators and have a seat on 
the council, then the longer this war goes, the more apparent it is that- had Severon been given 
another term- we would have been better-prepared for this war. Analyzed the enemy better.
The machine said that the PCA is all that humanity has, but I'm beginning to believe it was somehow
misinformed... or worse.

...But it wouldn't make a difference if the machine lied or not, would it?
Vaux would never surrender to humanity.
The people would have his head if he ever even tried.
```
