# MAGMOS 2
A battleground accessible only to the PCA. After the MDF (Magmos Dominion Force) launched an attack on several neighboring planets, the PCA closed off the planet and now fights the Magmosians in an all-out war. The Magmosians rely primarily on fear tactics, often being referred to as brutes and terrorists due to their cruelty and love of warfare- though their precise appearance outside of their machines and without their augmentations is currently unknown to all but a few officers.  

The war for Magmos 2 is an ongoing campaign in the server Armored Core: In The Ashes No More, exclusive to PCA characters
