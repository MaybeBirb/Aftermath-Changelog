30m tall MDF machine created for long-range support. All CRAWLERs are built with 10 legs, which are tightly packed, leading to a tall, tower-like stature.

CRAWLERs are nearly impossible to stagger due to their legs, and share similar health to HFLCs. When toppled, they take massively increased damage. Tripping them with long cables has proven extremely effective.

Typically, they are equipped with top-mounted laser sniper cannons that can roll to the bottom of the machine at close ranges. However, their lack of other weapons makes them serve primarily defensive, backline roles.
