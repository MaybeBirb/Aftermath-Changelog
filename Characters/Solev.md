# Solev, VAKETHORAN Captain
---
*"If your own self-importance takes precedence over ALL the lives of our future generations, then so be it. Go and make what you will of your life."*
- Solev, addressing VAKETHORAN gunner [Ahekohek](Ahekohek.md) aboard the VAKETHORAN

Solev was the captain of the MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN, and led the ship into Magmos's exosphere.

### History
Solev was an experienced Magmosian captain, and was assigned by Vaux Azzacci to use the VAKETHORAN to reach Magmos's exosphere and attract media attention from the rest of humanity.

### Traits and Equipment
Solev was cruel and harsh toward the Magmosians under his command, but ultimately cared deeply for the MDF, and was more than willing to sacrifice his life to ensure future generations of Magmosians could survive.

### Behind the Scenes
Solev was named after a recurring player in [Ish's](https://www.youtube.com/@ish) Minecraft Civilization videos, who continually appeared as a cold and ruthless dictator.
