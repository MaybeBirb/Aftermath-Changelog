# Tarkan, Head Researcher
---
*"Leave me be."*
- Tarkan, on Captain Ford asking if he was alright

Tarkan was the head researcher for the Central Prison.

### History
Tarkan was a researcher assigned to oversee scientific progress at the MDF Central Prison. During this time, Tarkan attempted to crack the screts of time travel; however, this resulted in a great many Deogans traveling from the past to the present. This caused heavy casualties, and Tarkan was transported to a safe bunker.  
Within this bunker, Tarkan was briefly confronted by the city defense coordinator, who yelled furiously at the researcher in Magmosian. After some time, a Venator entered, informing Tarkan something about Vaux Azzacci. Tarkan then left the bunker.

### Traits and Equipment
Tarkan wore a waist cape, and had standard Magmosian augmentations.

### Behind the Scenes
Tarkan was named after Crimson Knight Tarkon, a villain from a Dark Souls RP server.
