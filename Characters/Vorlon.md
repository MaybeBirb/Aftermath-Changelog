# Vorlon
---
*"Used to be grass fields, allegedly. There are still indents where there used to be rivers, but that was a long time ago."*
- Vorlon

Vorlon was a Magmosian guard assigned to the MDF Capital Prison.

### History
Assigned to protect the prisoners within the Capital Prison, Vorlon had an interaction with Beo Itou, where they briefly discussed the state of the planet. Later, Beo mentioned Vorlon in one of her data logs.

### Traits and Equipment
Vorlon had few distinguishable personality traits.

His augmentations were standard-issue Magmosian augmentations.

### Behind the Scenes
Vorlon was named after the [Vorlons](https://babylon5.fandom.com/wiki/Vorlon) from the TV series [Babylon 5](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Babylon_5).
