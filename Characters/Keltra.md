# Vespadon Keltra
---
*“Dominance is a natural trait of Magmosians.”*
*“So you believe Magmosians are naturally ordered toward superiority?”*
*“It is a logical conclusion from the data we have.”*
- Keltra and Viktor Xel

Keltra was a Vespadon of the MDF who was captured and interrogated by the PCA.

### History
While the details of her capture are unknown, Keltra was the first and only Vespadon to ever be captured by the PCA. Viktor Xel, at the time a Chief Warrant Officer, attempted to interrogate her to little success.

After giving up, Viktor ordered that Keltra be taken to her cell. However, before the order could be followed, Keltra killed the guards in the room and escaped, having knocked Viktor to the ground.

As a Vespadon, she proved extraordinarily difficult to kill. However, after a long period of chasing her and many dead GF soldiers, she was ultimately killed by a dual-minigun-equipped sentry.

### Traits and Equipment
Keltra was a professional ragebaiter of a Vespadon, speaking calmly with a hint of passive-aggressiveness and boredom.

She was equipped with white augmentations standard for Magmosian vespadons.

### Behind the Scenes
Keltra was named after a character from another universe.
