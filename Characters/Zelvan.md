# Battle Master Zelvan
---
*"Either way, you're no inmate. I'm taking you to Battle Master Zelvan, 𝘮𝘰𝘳𝘥𝘦𝘬𝘢𝘪 𝘥𝘳𝘢𝘬𝘢𝘳."*
- Unidentified Magmosian guard, to Hornet

Zelvan was the battle master, warden, and city defense coordinator for the Capital Prison.

### History
Assigned to defend the MDF Capital Prison, Zelvan was given a multitude of roles by the MDF, including being the prison's warden, being the city defense coordinator, and being the fortress's battle master. However, he chose the lattermost title to distinguish himself.

### Traits and Equipment
Zelvan was extremely hostile in his only on-screen appearance, confronting [Tarkan](Tarkan.md) about the Deogan time traveling incident.

He was equipped with customized Magmosian augmentations that game his armor sharper features.

### Behind the Scenes
Zelvan was unintentionally identified by multiple titles across his existence, leading to him being a very busy Magmosian.
