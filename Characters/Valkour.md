# Valkour, Magmosian Operative
---
*"You thought he was your friend... I thought he was annoying."*
- Valkour

Valkour was a Magmosian of unknown status, assigned as personal bodyguard to the MDF Head of Education.

### History
Valkour, a Magmosian Operative, was the personal bodyguard of the Head of Education at the time of the PCA strike team infiltrating the armored train. Though Valkour also headed security, he didn't join the resistance to the PCA strike team until the final car, where he killed the two remaining PCA GF soldiers from behind  while accompanied by an unidentified Magmosian soldier.

Despite his prowess, he found himself unable to stop the strike team, as he had several dorsal spines broken while paralyzed by stun rounds from Captain Ford. Although initially feigning helplessness, he attempted to launch a rocket into the ground at his feet to suicide-bomb the captain, but Ford was quick enough to shoot him in the head before this could happen.

### Traits and Equipment
Valkour was a callous individual who was self-proclaimed to not have much patience for humans. He was highly zealous, and in combat, demonstrated extreme agility and accuracy.

He was equipped with customized Magmosian augmentations, his armor being colored blue and orange, while he had several pipes along his upper body made for oxygen supplying in low-oxygen environments. He carried two laser handguns as his main weapons, though kept knives in reserve. His helmet, unlike most Magmosian helmets, was close-faced.

### Behind the Scenes
Valkour was named and designed after [Vapor](https://herofactory.fandom.com/wiki/Vapor) from [Hero Factory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lego_Hero_Factory), and more specifically, an interpretation of the character from the series The General.

His fighting style was based on the ]BX-Series Commando Droids](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/BX-series_droid_commando) from the [Clone Wars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Wars:_The_Clone_Wars) TV series.
