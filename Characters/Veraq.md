*"Hhh… kan verent krovel veknar kerr kyen-kae, vek kan verent kerr vokru vet-kov et feraux vek va-drakar?"*
- Veraq to [Deura](Deura.md)

Veraq was a Magmosian guard within the MDF Capital Prison.

### History
Veraq appeared exclusively in a conversation with Deura, heard through the Vespadon's door. From Hornet's translation, it could be inferred that the two were discussing humans not actually being so bad.
