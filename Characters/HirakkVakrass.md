# Hirakk Vakrass, Head of KT
---
*Another detail of note, however, was how abnormally **tall** they were. They looked like the equivalent of a 7’ human - putting Villarian to shame, and looking downward at Hornet with neutrality.*
- Description of Hirakk's height

Hirakk Vakrass is the current leader of the Magmosian Special Forces division, and is the wife of [Vaux Azzacci](VauxAzzacci.md).

### History
Hirakk's first appearance was in the background of a council meeting, where she approved a fight between two council members in her spouse's stead.

Later on, when Villarian returned to the MDF, Hirakk was the one to welcome him on arriving home. Later still, when Villarian requested to be able to meet with Hornet elsewhere, Hirakk agreed on the condition that she would accompany him.
Hornet asked Hirakk how Magmosians expressed affection in Public, and Hirakk gave a rather spartan answer.

### Traits and Equipment
Hirakk is extremely quite ans short-spoke, saying as little as possible in most situations. She has been described as cold and borderline emotionless, though her and Vaux Azzacci's affection for one another appears to be genuine.

Hirakk has black augmentations with purple rims, as well as a shoulder cape and waist cape. She is equipped with a physical twinblade.

### Behind the Scenes
Hirakk Vakrass, in Magmosian, roughly translates to "Quiet sort".
