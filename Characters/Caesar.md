# Magmosian Infiltrator Caesar
---
*”Vek tal dras vekra VAK THRALL, krass komvar 'Princess'.”*
- Caesar, attempting to kill Sarah Greywolf.

Caesar was the callsign of a Magmosian Infiltrator who boarded an MDF warship, in the hopes of killing a particular crew of pilots.

## History
Caesar was one of the Infiltrators initially tasked with boarding the PCA Heavy Warship housing the pilots who had investigated the HC Manufacturing Plant.

Although initially successful in killing the ship's captain, Caesar then attempted to kill Sarah, who- after a prolonged fight- ultimately tased the Infilatrator and killed him with a plasma shot to the head.

## Equipment
Caesar was equipped with the standard MDF Infiltrator arsenal, and wielded a singular Infiltrator sword.

## Behind the Scenes
Caesar, like the other Magmosian Infilatrators of his time, spoke in what is now referred to as Old Magmosian; a language no longer translatable due to GM incompetence. Caesar was intended to be the lead Infiltrator, but this was never stated in canon.
