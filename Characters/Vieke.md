# Vieke, Magmosian Infiltrator
---
*"If you have anything else to ask, ask it."*
- Vieke

Vieke was a Magmosian infiltrator assigned to kill Thanatos of the Grim Reapers, though she now works under him.

### History
During her time in the MDF, Vieke was married to an unidentified male Magmosian. Eventually, Vieke was smuggled to Cryonis about a PCA ship, where she was assigned to kill Thanatos.

While she successfully eliminated Executive Officer Hoarfrost, Vieke was ultimately unsuccessful in killing Thanatos due to him using a Polara blade. After her loss, she considered suicide bombing him, but eventually decided to temporarily work with him in order to have freedom of movement - under the terms she would not fight the MDF or directly help only the PCA.

At one point, Hornet came to Vieke in order to ask her how Magmosians proposed, and how they expressed affection in private.

### Traits and Equipment
Vieke is cold and short-spoken, often saying only as much as she deems necessary. She presents as hostile or emotionless, even to other Grim Reapers.

She has standard Magmosian Infiltrator augmentations that have now been painted black with red highlights, and she wields an Infiltrator sword.

### Behind the Scenes
The GM assumed Vieke would die fighting Thanatos.
