# Vaill
---
*"...Hornet, I apologize for Vaill’s behavior, but must you start a fight over it?"*
- Villarian

Vaill is a Magmosian solder within the MDF.

### History
While Hornet was attempting both training and exercise without Villarian in an MDF base, Vaill noticed her and approached her to watch. When she was finished sparring against a training dummy, Vaill incentivized a fight between the two, and Hornet took him up on it.  
The fight, however, was broken up by Villarian a short while in, and him and Hornet left shortly thereafter.

### Traits and Equipment
Vaill was aggressive and abrasive, with a natural disliking of humans.

He had standard Magmosian augmentations.

### Behind the Scenes
Vaill was named after an obscure character from a Dark Souls RP server.
