# Vaux Azzacci, Supreme Leader
---
*"Questions will yield you little."*
- Vaux Azzacci, in the Viscerian Grove

Vaux Azzacci is the supreme leader of the MDF, elected through meritous determination.

### History
Vaux Azzacci was elected to the position of supreme leader some time before humanity arrived on Magmos 2. From there, he led the war against the humans on the side of the MDF.

Several years into the war, the [Antisystem](Antisystem.md) hacked the Assembly of Allied Nations' servers, forcing a virtual meeting with Vaux Azzacci. The events of this meeting are unknown.

At an entirely later date, Vaux was seen in the Viscerian Grove, speaking with [PCA Major General Abel](MG-Abel.md) about something unclear. He interacted briefly with Ladybug, Outcast, and Seer Grim.

It is unknown when this happened, but at some point, Vaux Azzacci, in a display of strength to his people, killed a Magmosian Arena Beast on his own, without any internal weaponry.  

### Traits and Equipment
Vaux has been described as somewhat young and arrogant, though in confrontation, he has displayed mostly passive and collected traits. He cares about his people to a fault, having little regard for laws of engagement unless they suit the Magmosian definitions of honor. He is unfathomably powerful in combat, having killed a Magmosian Arena Beast on his own without the use of internal weaponry at one point.

Vaux has specialized black augmentations with gold trimmings, and wears a waist cape and shoulder cape. His primary weapons appear to be paired swords.

### Behind the Scenes
Vaux Azzacci's name means "Machine Killer". He was determined to be at a similar power level to Raw Steel, the Champion of Earth.
