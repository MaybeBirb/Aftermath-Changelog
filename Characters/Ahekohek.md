# Ahekohek
---
*"Hmh. Tai pervak tai ve thrall vak vakrit tarranov."*
- Ahekohek, apparently insulting [Villarian](Villarian.md)

Ahekohek was an MDF ship gunner for the MDF.

## History
Ahekohek appeared briefly within the PCA Central Prison, as one of the captured Magmosians. He, Hornet, and Villarian spoke for some time, throughout which Ahekohek insulted and mocked Villarian numerous times for his relationship with Hornet.

Ahekohek was later confirmed to be on board the MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN as its chief gunner.

## Behind the Scenes
Ahekohek was named after the Akohekohe, a rare bird endemic to Hawaii.
