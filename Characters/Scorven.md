# Scorven, War Council Member
---
*He’d never felt so much hypocrisy in his entire life.*
- Description of Scorven's thoughts in MDF Story 7

Scorven is a member of the Magmosian War Council in an unknown position.

### History
Scorven has only had one appearance, in which he, as a recent Magmosian to join the council, witnessed a disagreement between [Herenov Vaught](HerenovVaught.md) and [Vadersh](Vadersh.md), two other members of the War Council. This argument escalated into a legally-sanctioned duel, which ended quickly in Herenov's favor.

### Traits and Equipment
Scorven had few known traits, though generally disagreed with the status quo within the MDF. Regardless, he was too worried about death to speak up in opposition of it, despite the ability to do so being one of his role's defining traits.

Like all members of the War Council, he had black augmentations with a waist cape and a pointed shoulder cape.

### Behind the Scenes
Scorven had no speaking lines. He was named after [Soren the Architect](https://minecraftstorymode.fandom.com/wiki/Soren_the_Architect) from Minecraft: Story Mode.
