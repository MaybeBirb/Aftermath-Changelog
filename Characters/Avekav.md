# Magmosian Venator Avekav
---
*"Venators are guards. We like to protect things."*
- Avekav

Avekav was a Venator within the MDF army. He was deployed to the capital prison in order to protect Beo Itou.

## History
Avekav, like any Venator, was selected from the typical MDF soldier training pool for a unique desire to defend and protect other soldiers.

He was eventually sent to the MDF capital prison to aid the two Vespadons, [Deura](Deura.md) and [Thaddeus](Thaddeus.md), in regulating the inmates, and was assigned to protect the Chief Warrant Officer, Beo Itou. He engaged in conversation with her a number of times throughout their time in the prison.
When Beo and many other PCA officers were freed, Avekav was nowhere to be seen, likely having followed the other Venators to comply with standard emergency procedures.

## Traits and Equipment
Avekav tended toward the softer and kinder side, as is typical of Venators.

As a Venator, Avekav was equipped with typical Venator augmentations. While inside the prison, he was armed with a double-ended stun staff.

## Behind the Scenes
It was unclear whether or not Avekav was going to be shipped with Beo.
