# Viceroy, Regional Head of Security
---
*"Withdraw? Please, you only just got here. I would not want you to feel 𝘶𝘯𝘸𝘦𝘭𝘤𝘰𝘮𝘦."*
- Viceroy

Viceroy was a Magmosian infiltrator, and head of security for the region involving the Magma Dam and the MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN.

### History
Early into the war, Viceroy was appointed as the leader of the MDF Magma Dam's defenses, and as supervisor of the entire region's security. During this time, the Magma Dam managed to destroy a PCA fleet, including a Heavy Flagship.

When the dam was destroyed by PCA forces, Viceroy retained his position as regional security head, later being reassigned to guard the MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN during reparation efforts. As time progressed, Viceroy slowly loosened security out of boredom, hoping to lure PCA forces inside.  
However, this proved to be his downfall, as a PCA strike team later encountered him and neutralized his reinforcements by way of EMP. Despite Viceroy putting up a fight after his augmentations were shut down, he perished at the hands of the team on The System.

### Traits and Equipment
Viceroy suffered from extreme boredom and impatience during his time overseeing the VAKETHORAN. Due to a lack of action and an overuse of paperwork, he became somewhat reckless in engagements, and loved to talk.

He had standard Magmosian Infiltrator augmentations with a waist cape, and used a Partisan as his weapon of choice.
