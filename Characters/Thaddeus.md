# Thaddeus, Magmosian Vespadon
---
*"We ask that you're polite with Versca for the duration of her stay. I understand she's unfamiliar, but please don't obstruct her."*
- Thaddeus, introducing [Versca](Versca.md) to the Capital Prison

Thaddeus was one of the two Vespadons assigned to the MDF capital prison. He was rarely seen.

### History
During his time as a Vespadon within the MDF, Thaddeus was assigned to protect and interrogate the prisoners of the MDF Capital Prison. However, [Deura](Deura.md), the other Vespadon within the prison, took over most of the interrogating.  

Thaddeus was seen briefly introducing Versca, the royal Vespadon visiting from [Vaux Azzacci](VauxAzzacci.md). However, he was not encountered while the human strike team was extracting prisoners.

### Equipment
Thaddeus had standard Vespadon augmentations.

### Behind the Scenes
Thaddeus was named after [Jude the Apostle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jude_the_Apostle), commonly identified as Thaddeus. However, at the time of his creation, the GM did not remember that was where the name was from, and only remembered that the name was from somewhere.

Thaddeus was meant to be encountered by the players while exploring the Capital Prison's basement.
