# Vattelen Vella
---
*"Before Vaux Azzacci was our leader, we had Severon Vaught. Before that, Vattelen Vella. She oversaw the creation of the KR machines."*
- [Viceroy](Viceroy.md)

Vattelen Vella was a former leader of the MDF.

### History
Vattelen preceeded [Severon Vaught](SeveronVaught.md), and during her time as leader, created the KR-series MDF machines. These include the MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN and the MDF-KR01 WORLD EATER.

### Traits and Equipment
Unknown

### Behind the Scenes
Her name roughly translates to "Specific Time".
