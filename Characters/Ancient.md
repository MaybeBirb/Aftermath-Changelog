# Ancient
---
*"Valket Ancient, vvekra kerr ek-kov thraa. Telkevan."*
- An unidentified Magmosian soldier

Ancient was a Magmosian who was briefly mentioned during the mission **Destroy the MDF Armored Train**.

## History
Any details about Ancient are unknown due to the brevity of their mention, though it is presumed they were on board the MDF armored train at the time of its beseigement by PCA infiltration forces.

## Traits and Equipment
Unknown

## Behind the Scenes
In the mission description, it was mentioned that there were two council members to be killed, though only one was ever found- and their respective bodyguard, [MDF Operative Valkour](Valkour.md), was fought. Ancient was intented to be a second MDF operative, assigned to protect the other council member, but this was cut for mission brevity.
