# Magmosian Infiltrator Celian
---
*”I'm certain Celian would have been ecstatic if he was taken prisoner instead. But I've little curiosity for humankind."”*
- [Villarian](Villarian.md), on being asked if he had any questions

Celian was one of the Magmosian Infiltrators who attacked the Heavy Warship. He was noted to have been fascinated with humanity.

## History
Prior to his being an Infiltrator, Celian first appeared on a patrol alongside Villarian, where he was referenced to have aided Villarian in taking down a damaged PCA MT on the battlefield. He and Villarian were promoted to Infiltrators after this.

In a separate battlefield patrol, he and [Pharaoh](Pharaoh.md) discovered a human baby (1yo) in the compartment of a destroyed LC. It is unclear what the two Magmosians did with this.

Selected as one of the Infiltrators to board the PCA Warship housing the pilots from the HC Manufacturing Plant, Celian initially moved to attack Ausbruch, doing what he could to take the EKDROMOI pilot out. However, ultimately, Ausbruch rammed a Forklift into the Infilatrator, detonating the grenades Celian had and killing him instantly.

### Legacy
Celian was mentioned by [Villarian](Villarian.md) several times after his death, being referenced as a fanatic for human culture. He was the first seen Magmosian to speak English.

## Equipment
Celian was equipped with standard MDF Infiltrator augmentations, and an Infiltrator sword.

## Behind the Scenes
Celian, like the other Magmosian Infilatrators of his time, spoke in what is now referred to as Old Magmosian; a language no longer translatable due to GM incompetence.
