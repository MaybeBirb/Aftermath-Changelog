# Severon Vaught
---
*Attachment is not ended by loss, it is ended by you.*
- Severon Vaught, according to [Villarian](Villarian.md)

Severon Vaught is a former leader of the Magmos Dominion Force, and currently leads the Venators.

### History
Before [Vaux Azzacci](VauxAzzacci.md] became the leader of the MDF, Severon Vaught led the Magmosians. While what he did during his time as leader is unclear, it is possible he made the MDF move underground after they saw humanity.

Eventually, Severon's term as leader expired, and Vaux was found to be more meritous. Regardless, Severon was offered a position as leader of the Venators, and now has a seat on the War Council.

Severon's leadership was preceeded by [Vattelen Vella](VattelenVella.md).

### Traits And Appearance
Severon was noted to be much wiser than the current leader of the MDF, and was also described as somewhat "timid" in a data log. His equipment is unknown, but as War Council members have black augmentations with color-coded trimmings, it can be assumed that Severon has black, augmented armor with yellow trimmings to symbolize his status as leader of the Venators.

### Behind the Scenes
Severon Vaught was one of several Magmosians created while designing [Vaux Azzacci's](VauxAzzacci.md) war council.
