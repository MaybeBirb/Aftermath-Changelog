# Magmosian Infiltrator Pharaoh
---
*"Vak thrall vekra tor vek Pharaoh vakas threl. Krass nar."*
- [Celian](Celian.md), referencing Pharaoh in Old Magmosian

Pharaoh was an MDF infiltrator and sniper.

### History
Pharaoh was a Magmosian selected to join the Infiltrators at a reasonably young age. He briefly accompanied Celian when they found a human baby inside a destroyed LC's cockpit compartment, though what became of that child is yet unknown.

Eventually, Pharaoh was sent to attack the PCA warship housing the team that had investigated the HC manufacturing plant. Pharaoh, after having carried out some other task, arrived in the middle of the fight in the hangar, and began to provide sniper support.
On seeing Caesar's death, Pharaoh shot Sarah Graywolf through the chest, puncturing a lung. Ahosrtly thereafter he leaped down from his sniper perch, only searching briefly for other officers before Hornet successfully entered and activated her LC. Despite Pharaoh surrendering, Hornet quickly disposed of him with an LC slug shotgun round.

### Traits and Equipment
Pharaoh was both sentimental and tempermental, reacting strongly to the deaths of his comrades.

He was equipped with typical Magmosian infiltrator augmentations, and preferred to use a heavy laser sniper rifle as his primary form of offense. However, he also carried a standard Infiltrator sword.

### Behind the Scenes
As with all of the other Magmosian infiltrators aboard the Heavy Warship, Pharaoh spoke in Old Magmosian, a language that is now un-translatable due to the GM's incompetence.
