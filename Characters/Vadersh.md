# Vadersh, Minster of Public Works
---
*"Then you are blind. We have no need of a blind council"*
*"Nor have we need of a deaf one."*
- [Herenov Vaught](HerenovVaught.md) and Vadersh, shortly before dueling

Vadersh was the MDF minister of public works and a member of its War Council.

### History
Vadersh appeared only briefly, where she had a disagreement with fellow council member Herenov Vaught about funding and training schedules. Peculiarly, the Head of Education was nowhere to be seen at this time, leaving Vadersh to argue in her place. This disagreement turned into a legally-sanctioned duel with the permission of [Hirakk Vakrass](HirakkVakrass.md), which Vadersh lost when she received a slash to the throat. Herenov requested her successor be notified of Vadersh's passing.

### Traits and Equipment
Vadersh expressed concern for the Magmosian people on several occasions throughout her appearance. She was sharp in her points, but ultimately spoke with the preservation of life in mind.

She carried a typical Magmosian sword, and had black augmented armor with a waist cape and a shoulder cape.
