# Liekov
---
*"Vek drakar?"*
- Liekov, inquiring why [Villarian](Villarian.md) had brought Hornet along

Liekov was an unaugmented Magmosian, serving as the clerk at a front desk in one Magmosian official building.

### History
While Villarian was entering a central building to be taken in for questioning, Liekov was the Magmosian at the front desk. While he inquired why Hornet was accompanying Villarian, he presented himself as far more curious than hostile, and assured Villarian he would keep an eye on her while Villarian was being questioned.

He did not, however, interfere when [Vaill](Vaill.md) challenged Hornet.

### Traits and Equipment
Liekov was a curious Magmosian who carried little aggression, possibly a symptom of his lack of augmentations.

He wore armor not dissimilar to Magmosian combat augmentations, though all external.

### Behind the Scenes
Liekov was the first unaugmented Magmosian to be named, and only the second to appear on-screen.
