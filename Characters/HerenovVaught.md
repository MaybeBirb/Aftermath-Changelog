# Herenov Vaught, Head of Economics Management
---
*We would like to clarify that [...] these sanctions won’t have any actual economic impact, and enforcement isn’t particularly possible right now… However, it’s the thought that counts, and we have long delayed this course of action.*
- Herenov, in a statement to the PCA

Herenov Vaught is the head of economics management for the PCA, and a member of the War Council.

### History
Herenov's primary appearance was in the war council chambers, where he disagreed with the head of public works on how education funds were to be spent. The head of education was strangely absent from this discussion, but the debate eventually came down to a legally-approved fight, which Herenov won immediately with a slash to the throat.

In a later statement, Herenov announced ludicrous sanctions and economic restrictions on the PCA as an act of spite.

### Traits and Equipment
Throughout his appearances, Herenov has been irritable and somewhat sadistic, while making sardonic or flat jokes all the while.

Herenov has black MDF augmentations, with red lining along it. He also wears a shoulder cape and waistcloth, and wields paired swords.

### Behind the Scenes
Herenov was originally created for a meme in the server, though was later canonized in a short story. His last name, Vaught, being shared with [Severon Vaught](SeveronVaught.md) is pure coincidence.
