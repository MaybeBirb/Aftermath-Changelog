# Crest
---
*“I took what I know of localized peace and applied it on a larger scale. But… it felt very… nice. I feel as though I would enjoy it significantly more than the war that we already have, but surely, this is simply my mind playing a cruel joke on me.”*
- Crest

Crest was a new Magmosian soldier, who was ultimately killed by a Veldaron on orders of the Vespadon [Versca](Versca.md).

### History
Upon becoming an MDF soldier, but before his first battle, Crest was noted to be having nightmares about the battle ahead. However, these nightmares turned into dreams avout peace, eventually.

Though he consulted the Vespadon of his regiment (that being Versca) frequently, she ultimately assigned a Veldaron to kill him due to his increasing desire for the peace he dreamed about. Crest was killed while resting on his bed.

### Traits and Equipment
Crest was a fairly nervous and skittish individual, in spite of his attempts to stay calm and collected. He had dreams about peace, which he enjoyed, and he tolerated hypotheticals rather easily. However, he was prone to shakiness at the time of his appearance.

Equipment unknown.

### Behind the Scenes
Although initially only a semi-canon character, Crest was retroactively made canon by the declaration of all the short stories to be canon.
