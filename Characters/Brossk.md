# Magmosian Leader Brossk
---
*"They say Brossk tore the theocratic Falorian leader’s still-beating heart from his chest after fighting him to the death with his bare hands, despite giving the Falorian his weapons. They say he put every Deogan king in a pit and fought them all at once, yet still emerged victorious. They say it was him who instated true Dominion."*
- [Villarian](Villarian.md), in a non-canon description of Brossk

Brossk was the creator of the Magmos Dominion Force, and its first leader. He was alive during the war with the Deogans and the Falorians.

### History
Brossk was the first leader of the MDF, having established it in order to unite the Magmosians against the two other races of his time, the expansionist Deogans and the theocratic Falorians.

Though his exploits are largely unknown, it has been revealed that he destroyed a reasonably large Deogan encampment on his own, much to the dismay of his second-in-command, [Heer](Heer.md).

It is rumored that, near the end of the war with the Deogans and Falorians, Brossk killed the Falorian leader after defeating him in combat (where the Falorian had his weapons and Brossk did not), and he killed the Deogan kings all fighting him at once.

### Traits and Equipment
Brossk was subject to Magmosian augmentations, though these augmentations predated the modern era's rendition, being referenced to have white armor plating. Though Brossk wielded a physical twinblade in his only canon appearance, he was stated to dislike using it, preferring fighting with his fists over the weapon.

### Behind the Scenes
Brossk was named after the GM's first D&D character.
