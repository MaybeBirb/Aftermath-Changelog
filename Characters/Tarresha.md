# Tarresha
---
*"Bullsh#t."*
- The only English Tarresha has ever spoken.

Tarresha is a Magmosian Drelanx within the MDF, and is [Villarian's](Villarian.md) sister.

### History
Tarresha was initially mentioned as Villarian's sister when Hornet asked if Villarian had any family.  

Later, Villarian took Hornet to meet Tarresha, and the three had a brief exchange in Magmosian. Later that same day, Tarresha was assigned to keep Villarian on-time and productive during Hornet's visit to the MDF, interrupting the two by breaking down the door to yell at Villarian to hurry up.

### Traits and Equipment
Tarresha, like any Drelanx, is rude, impulsive, and reckless. While she is fond of her brother, Villarian, she is also extremely aggressive, and can't manage her strength very well.

She is equipped with standard Drelanx augmentations, and is therefore significantly taller than most Magmosians.

### Behind the Scenes
Tarresha was originally mentioned by Villarian as being in the "destroyer corps", though this was later renamed to be the Drelanxes.
