# Sven
---
*"I had meant getting along with one another. But there are a few jobs to keep you all entertained."*
- Sven

Sven was an MDF guard within the Capital Prison.

### History
Sven was a Magmosian guard who was assigned to protect the prisoners in the Capital Prison. During one lunch break, he interacted with Lieutenant Arno Jager.

During the prison break, Sven was nowhere to be seen.

### Traits and Equipment
Sven had few distinguishable personality traits, but was generally somewhat of a gently giant whenever he appeared.

Sven had typical Magmosian augmentations, though was significantly taller than most Magmosians.

### Behind the Scenes
Sven was one of a large wave of Magmosians created exclusively for the MDF Capital Prison.
