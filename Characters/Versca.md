# Versca, Magmosian Vespadon
---
*"You should get some rest. The battle is going to be long, proper rest is a necessity."*
- Versca, to [Crest](Crest.md)

Versca is a Magmosian Vespadon within the MDF, who was later promoted to one of [Vaux Azzacci's](VauxAzzacci.md) personal Vespadons.

### History
Versca was the Vespadon of an unidentified company of Magmosians, acting as their therapist. During this time, she had to eliminate various Magmosians for thought crimes, including [Crest](Crest.md), a soldier having dreams about peace.

At some point, she was promoted to be one of Vaux Azzacci's personal Vespadons, and briefly visited the MDF Capital Prison. What she did during her time there is unknown.

### Traits and Equipment
Versca was soft-spoken and humorous, like all Vespadons. While royal Vespadon, she presented as a little shy.

Versca had standard Magmosian augmentations, and as royal vespadon, she had a waist cape and a shoulder cape.
