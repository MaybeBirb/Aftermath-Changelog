Small drones typically deployed in droves by the MDF. These drones are incredibly simple-minded, even to the point of firing at flares, and are painted white and extremely thin. However, they tend to crawl as their main form of movement.

With the health of a small tank, these drones are easy to destroy individually. Though they are pass-produced by HIVE LEADERs.

The drones have two primary weapons, those being small, chest-mounted machine guns and single-cell missile launchers. =

### HIVE DEFENDER
A variant of Drone that has integrated circular saws for hands, and has darker armor.
