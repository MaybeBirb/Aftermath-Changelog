One of the KR-series machines created by Vattelen Vella. The WORLD EATER was a massive machine, almost as large as a mountain.

At the time of its deployment, the WORLD EATER had been stripped out into a rock-filled shell, and was used as bait by the MDF to lure PCA officers in. Its actual capabilities before this were unknown.

### BEHIND THE SCENES  
The WORLD EATER was conceptualized as an enormous, STRIDER-sized, bear-like machine that took three entire days to activate, and would broadcast a ghostly red sigil in the sky to indicate how many days remained. Originally, the plan was for a fight similar to the ICE WORM, but with an opponent the size of the STRIDER.

This idea was scrapped for two reasons:  
1. The PCA would most likely just strike the WORLD EATER with heavy artillery during its charging stage.  
2. This risked make the ICE WORM encounter feel underwhelming.
