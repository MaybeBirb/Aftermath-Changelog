A variant of BELLIGERENT units created to defend the Antisystem. Shares proportions with standard BELLIGERENTs.

The armor of OBVERSE units is nearly identical to BELLIGERENTs, though their heads are larger, and they have greater mobility. They could fold into walls.

Uniquely, OBVERSE units are unpiloted. The only OBVERSE units ever seen were equipped with stun maces.

The System suspected these units were only made to deter smaller investigation squads from the Antisystem's core bunker.
