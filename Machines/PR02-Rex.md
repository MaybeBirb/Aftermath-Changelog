50m machine controlled by an artificial intelligence for stealth operations and kidnapping. REX machines aren't very damage-resistant, but are still quite durable overall.

The body of a REX machine is covered in miniguns with suppressors on each barrel, evidence of overengineering on the part of the MDF. Otherwise, they are equipped with dual grenade cannons on their shoulders, a stun net launcher, and two large frontal claws.

Because of their outstanding capacity against both singular and grouped targets, direct engagement is not advised.
