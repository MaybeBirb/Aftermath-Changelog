FURNACEs are 12m tall, hovering machines with six arms. Typically, two of these arms are flamethrowers, while two wield melee weapons and two hold ranged weapons (often machine guns).

They have relatively little AP and stability, and can be dispathed easily.

FURNACE units can fold into walls of internal structures, and are designed to perform both maintenance and defense within long-standing structures.
