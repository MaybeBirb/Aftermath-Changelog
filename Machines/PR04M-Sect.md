MT-sized machines reminiscent of a praying mantiss. They have low AP individually, but are frequently deployed in swarms.

SECTs are capable of extremely rapid flight, flying and landing as what has been described as an oncoming swarm of locusts. Their main weapons are their two frontal claws.

As one of three variation on the PR04 template, these are speculated to comprise about a third of all PR04 machines.
