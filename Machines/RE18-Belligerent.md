* 16m
* Magmos Dominion Force machine reverse-engineered from *AA18: Light Cavalry*
* Uses thrusters like an LC, but is far faster with its legs and is even capable of rolling
* After external armor is broken, the inside can be harmed. Damage to limbs destroys them
* Has a shield that, when overheated, keeps up but locks the arm in place
* Has the advantages of physical strength, durability, and stability over LCs
* Weaker on aerial combat, detection systems, and damage potential than LCs


### OBSERVED WEAPONRY
**TYPE-A**  
-Flame Sword  
-Long-Range Assault Rifle  
-Repeating Shotgun  
-Linear Sniper Rifle  
-Kinetic Heat Shield  
-Shoulder Spreak Bazooka  
-Shoulder Support Missiles  
-Supporting Jetpack  

**TYPE-B**  
-Heavy Minigun  
-Repeating Rocket Launcher
