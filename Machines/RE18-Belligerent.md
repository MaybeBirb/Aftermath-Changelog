A 16m MDF machine reverse-engineered from *AA18: Light Cavalry*, and now used as the rank-and-file of the MDF. Though they use thrusters like an LC, BELLIGERENTs are far faster with their legs and are even capable of rolling, along with other advanced maneuvers that would ordinarily put too much strain on an LC's joints. Research indicates this is possible due to Redsteel joints.  

These machines are plated in armor. After this external armor is broken, the inside can be harmed.  
BELLIGERENTs have the advantages of physical strength, maneuverability, and stability over LCs. However, they are weaker on aerial combat, detection systems, and overall durability.

Several distinct BELLIGERENT variants have been seen.

### ALPHA-CLASS  
Standard-issue BELLIGERENT units.
### BETA-CLASS  
Heavy BELLIGERENT units with double-layered armor on all areas but the head.
### GAMMA-CLASS  
Lightly-armored BELLIGERENT units with jetpacks.
### OBVERSE-CLASS
A unique, special subtype of BELLIGERENT that was used to defend the Antisystem's central processor. Technically a separate machine. (See: [MDF-IN08 OBVERSE](IN08-Obverse.md))
