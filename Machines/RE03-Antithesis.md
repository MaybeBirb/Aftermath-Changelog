# MDF-RE03 ANTITHESIS
---
[NO IMAGE]

---

The core component of the Antisystem, which included all of the processing units. Only one ANTITHESIS was ever created, and it was a tri-former.

This machine was destroyed by an elite team led by Captain Sarah Graywolf, though ultimately led to her being discharged due to deteriorating mental conditions. Throughout the encounter, the Antisystem made antagonizing comments to the assembled PCA agents that left lasting mental effects.

### CORE MODE
A rectangle-shaped block form that's heavily armored from all sides, with a singular red "eye" light in the middle. No offensive capabilities are known about this form, as it seems to optimize processing and transmission.

### ENFORCEMENT MODE
A form made to replicate the PCA's AAP03: ENFORCER. Fully armored in armor similar to BELLIGERENT units, the Enforcement Mode was equipped with a large cannon, a ten-way split missile launcher, and a pile bunker.

### TERMINATION MODE
A form that resembles the Lava Tarragon, covered in dense armor taken from the floor of the combat chamber. This form is enormous, and has pulse wings that can be fulled with burning napalm and flung at opponents for a wall of fire. Vertical missile rows are present along this form's back, and it can use both its tail and claws as kinetic weapons. In later stages of the fight, it could utilize a long-range plasma jet from its "mouth", a continuous beam that could cut the bunker walls and ceiling easily.
