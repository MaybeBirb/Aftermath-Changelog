# MACHINES
---  
Craft piloted by the PCA, the AIM, or the MDF that have thus far been exclusive to the Magmos campaign.

## MDF
### CL Series
Standard-issue, rank-and-file MDF machines. Typically composes the bulk of their lighter forces from before the PCA invasion.
- [MDF-CL05 HERMIT](CL05-Hermit.md)
- [MDF-CL06 CRAWLER](CL06-Crawler.md)
- [MDF-CL24 ISOPOD](CL24-Isopod.md)
### DR Series
Autonomous drone weapons built to overrun PCA defenses in droves. Currently, all Hive Leaders have been destroyed, meaning no DR Series units remain.
- [MDF-DR01 DRONE](DR01-Drone.md)
- [MDF-DR06 HIVE LEADER](DR06-Hive-Leader.md)
### IN Series
Internal defense machines for repelling intruders in priority locations. Some internal structures are built to house these inside their walls.
- [MDF-IN02 FURNACE](IN02-Furnace.md)
- [MDF-IN08 OBVERSE](IN08-Obverse.md)
### RE Series
Entirely reverse-engineered machines from the PCA.
- [MDF-RE03 ANTITHESIS](RE03-Antithesis)
- [MDF-RE18 BELLIGERENT](RE18-Belligerent.md)
### PR Series
Predator-series machines that specialize in attacking singular targets without getting distracted. Sometimes autonomous, sometimes piloted.
- [MDF-PR02 REX](PR02-Rex.md)
- MDF-PR04
-   [MDF-PR04M SECT](PR04M-Sect.md)
-   MDF-PR04H
- [MDF-PR06 CERATO](PR06-Cerato.md)
- [MDF-PR08 RAPTOR](PR08-Raptor.md)
### UM Series
Unmanned machines at a power level similar to OMACs. They typically fight large groups of PCA officers with little difficulty.
- [Unmanned Machine A](UMA.md)
- [Unmanned Machine B](UMB.md)
- [Unmanned Machine C](UMC.md)
- [Unmanned Machine D](UMD.md)
### KR Series
Machines created as a "last line of defense" for the MDF, to be used when it was in dire straits. Very old, but the resources taken to build these have made them unfathomably powerful.
- [MDF-KR01 WORLD EATER](KR01-World-Eater.md)
- [MDF-KR12 VAKETHORAN](KR12-Vakethoran.md)
### WS Series
Aircraft used by the MDF, usually in the form of ships or smaller jets.
- [MDF-WS02 BATTLESHIP](WS02-Battleship.md)
- [MDF-WS05 GUNSHIP](WS05-Gunship.md)
- [MDF-WS09 BRACER](WS09-Bracer.md)
- [MDF-WS12 WARSHIP_RS](WS12-Warship-Rs.md)
