Pre-history  
```
08/03/XXXX: AAN researchers located and named Magmos 2 from an observatory on UNSPECIFIED. Subsequent reports identified the planet’s composition.
```
## Year 1  
```
05/04/XXXX: AAN specialists successfully made landfall on Magmos 2 for the first time, 14 years after discovery.
06/12/XXXX: Discovery of Redsteel.
06/30/XXXX: The PCA is deployed to Magmos 2 to establish a Closure Barrier, citing hostile conditions not safe for public access.
09/29/XXXX: AAN mining habitations are constructed. Magmos 2 is officially declared a mining planet.
12/10/XXXX: Redsteel extraction is fully streamlined. Contact is opened for workers to ALLMIND and several other markets upon agreement to an NDA.
```
## Year 2  
```
03/15/XXXX: ALLMIND sales from Magmos 2 slowly increase. An investigation finds no illegal activity. The System logs the abnormal activity.
09/01/XXXX: Initial reports of anomalous activity within certain mineshafts. Reports flagged as seismic activity by The System; safety measures are scheduled.
12/18/XXXX: First contact with the Magmos Dominion Force. Negotiations are attempted, but fall through on the first attempt. MDF forces initiate hostilities across the globe.
12/19/XXXX: The Assembly of Allied Nations is briefed on the developing situation on Magmos 2, and officially declares war on the Magmosians one day after the initial attack.
12/19/XXXX-12/21/XXXX: Miners evacuated.
12/20/XXXX: Magmos 2 officially declared fully illegal to enter. Existing passes issued to civilians are revoked and exchanged for equivalent benefits.
12/23/XXXX: Major General Abel is assigned to Magmos 2.
12/25/XXXX: Negotiations re-attempted for a Christmas truce. Negotiations fell through a second time without casualties.
12/28/XXXX: Construction of a Closure System begins.
```
## Year 3  
```
01/26/XXXX: MDF Orbital Cannons are revealed from various ore-deficient mountaintops, preventing the establishment of a Closure System.
03/29/XXXX: Construction of a Closure System halts, citing cost issues and part manufacturing errors.
05/11/XXXX: Specific, experienced PCA regiments deployed to Magmos 2 to end the war quickly. Attempts fail due to the MDF resistance increasing.
05/11/XXXX: PCA presence increases on Magmos in the public eye, identifying the additional forces as a replacement for the cancelled Closure System.
05/23/XXXX: Magmosian Infiltrators first begin appearing and killing important PCA personnel. Safety plans are revised immediately.
05/31/XXXX: First-ever Magmosian soldier captured. Study of internal weaponry, biology, and culture begins.
09/18/XXXX: Construction of the ACC87 Super-Heavy Mobile Carrier “Siren” authorized, alongside an additional troop deployment.
11/24/XXXX: Deployment of external emergency rescue squadron to Siren. (First player mission on Magmos)
12/28/XXXX: Magmos is declassified to all PCA personnel. PCA presence on Magmos officially becomes the second-greatest in the galaxy.
```
## Year 4  
```
01/03/XXXX: First aggressive traitor, Colonel Bloodhound, is identified and killed in action along with his squad.
01/22/XXXX: Contact with an HC manufacturing plant is lost.
01/25/XXXX: First contact is made with the Lava Tarragon.
01/25/XXXX: Attempted assassination of priority targets by the MDF (first appearance of Infiltrators).
02/17/XXXX: First CATAPHRACT-RD is constructed and scheduled for field testing, being assigned to 1st Captain Ford.
03/14/XXXX: Diversification of MDF opponents; first encounter with BELLIGERENT units and MDF-WS12 Redsteel Warships. 1st Captain Ford is captured.
03/30/Xxxx: All HIVE LEADERs destroyed in a large-scale offensive.
04/09/XXXX: Following unsuccessful air raids, the first MDF-PCA alliance is created to kill the Lava Tarragon. This attempt ultimately results in failure, and the alliance is discontinued.
04/29/XXXX: Operation Iota IV. First encounter with the Antisystem. SP Warrant Officer Beo Itou is captured.
05/05/XXXX: Majority of PCA forces evacuate Magmos to focus on other areas while PCA intelligence locates the Antisystem.
05/05/XXXX: PCA presence decreases on Magmos 2. No public explanation is given, but it seems to be linked to Operation Total Crusade.
05/12/XXXX: Beo Itou gives the MDF the Transmitter Patterns and Earth Codes.
06/13/XXXX: The MDF-KR01 WORLD EATER is falsely identified as a WMD, leading to the capture of 1st Lieutenant Arno Jager and Warrant Officer Stockholm.
08/21/XXXX: PCA intelligence locates the Antisystem and begins to formulate a plan and collect data.
08/29/XXXX: First Magmosian Infiltrator found outside of Magmos 2, located on Cryonis, attempting to assassinate Colonel Thanatos.
09/05/XXXX: The PCA returns to Magmos. A special operations unit is deployed to infiltrate the MDF prison, breaking out Ford, Beo, Stockholm, and Jager.
10/14/XXXX: Archon Squad deployed to break through the ASDF and achieves a miraculous victory.
10/16/XXXX: Special team deployed to destroy the Antisystem, which is successful. Antisystem is destroyed at the cost of Sergeant Xiphoid and CS1stC Beast’s lives.
10/16/XXXX: The AAN allocates additional resources, including AIM reinforcements, DA-5 and K4-K3E bombs, and a second ACC87 Super-Heavy Mobile Carrier.
11/04/XXXX: AIM ships deployed to Magmos 2’s orbit. Documents leaked concerning the use of DA-5 and K4-K3E.
12/08/XXXX: Reinforcements deployed to support the two ACC87 Super-Heavy Mobile Carriers coming under an unprecedented attack.
```
