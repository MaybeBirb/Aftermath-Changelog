- 1 extra OMAC Credit if all anti-air artillery is destroyed

**Mission Objectives**  
> Only available to members of Archon Team or The Grim Reapers  
- Break through any and all MDF defenses  
- __For your own safety, ignore all casualties and wounded__  
+ Supported by friendly craft

**Mission description**  
```
Our return to Magmos 2 has begun. This will be the first of many missions in our campaign to destroy the MDF machine designated ‘the Antisystem’.
The Dominion Force has taken the time we were away to entrench themselves and build a line of defense around where we have discovered the Antisystem to be. They have constructed an enormous defensive wall, and AntiSystem Defensive Formation (ASDF), composed of multiple elements.

Anti-air turrets to stop air raids. Pulse shield missiles to intercept artillery. The wall is 360 degrees around the enemy base, and long-range laser cannons won't allow Warships to get close. A ground assault is a horrific idea and would lead to countless casualties... but it is our only option.

They have sniper cannons searching for officers attempting to retrieve the wounded. Do not stop moving under any circumstances. Injured personnel are to stay as low as possible and get into trenches and ditches if available, and will be recovered when the battle is over.

It's suicide, but it's our only hope... and it's why we're sending you.
Godspeed, Reapers and Archon Team.
```
